About Grace Tech Solutions

Grace Tech Solutions (GTS) is an authorised dealer for essential electrical and automation components demanded by Indian industry. We connect production floors with certified switchgear, control gear, cabling, guarding, and monitoring systems from globally trusted OEM partners.

Our team aligns every delivery with compliance, uptime, and safety targets—pairing genuine parts with knowledgeable application support. From greenfield build-outs to retrofit upgrades, we help plants standardise on dependable components that keep operations online.
